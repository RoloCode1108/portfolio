Hello Season Ticket Holders!
Here are your Season Ticket Holder Updates. 
2022 is Around the Corner!
In case you missed it, you should have received your 2022 renewal information on July 19th. If you did not receive the email, please contact your account manager. 
Astros Membership Enrollment Program
The Astros Membership Enrollment Program provides our Season Ticket Holders with great opportunities and benefits including: 
  • 15% off in the Team Store
  • A STH Loyalty card 
  • A Flexible Payment Plan
  • Opportunity to Cheer Now, Pay Later for Postseason*
If you are not already on an AME plan, you may sign up at the link below. Reach out to your rep with any questions you may have. 
Sign Up Now
*Not all accounts on AME are eligible for Cheer Now, Pay Later.
 
Update your Default Card
If you need to update your default card on file before a payment is run, you can now update your card information on your myTickets account. Go to astros.com/mytickets and click on the 'Payment Tab' to update your card. If you need assistance, please contact your account manager. 
 
Postseason Information is Coming Soon
As the end of the regular season approaches, we are working to finalize Postseason details. Be on the lookout for upcoming information regarding Postseason pricing, how to secure your tickets, and individual presale opportunities. 
Season Ticket Holder Spotlight - Submit a Nominee
Each month, we will highlight a notable Season Ticket Holder for their great attitude and passion for the Astros. If you know a fellow STH that you would like to spotlight for their Astros fandom, please send an email to your account manager with your nomination and reasons for selection. Then, be on the lookout next month to see if they were selected.
 
If you have any questions regarding your renewal or any of the information covered in this email, please reach out to your account manager for help.
